GOES-16 Videos, Northern New Mexico

This page shows near real-time satellite imagery of northern New Mexico. The imagery has been acquired by NOAA's GOES-16 satellite and processed by Descartes Labs. The left panels show visible light. The right panels show thermal emission, from the ground or from fires, as seen in infrared light. All videos are updated hourly. The format of these videos is subject to change, and their ongoing availability is not guaranteed.
