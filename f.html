GOES-16 Videos, Northern New Mexico

This page shows near real-time satellite imagery of northern New Mexico. The imagery has been acquired by NOAA's GOES-16 satellite and processed by Descartes Labs.

The left panels show visible light: a red band, a green band derived from other bands, and a blue band. The right panels show infrared light sensitive to thermal emission from the earth's surface.

All videos are updated hourly. The format of these videos is subject to change, and their ongoing availability is not guaranteed.
